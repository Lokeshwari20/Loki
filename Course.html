About course

Web development refers to the process of creating, building, and maintaining websites or web applications. It involves a combination of coding, design, and content creation to produce functional, visually appealing, and user-friendly digital experiences. Web development encompasses everything from simple static pages to complex dynamic applications, e-commerce platforms, and social networks.
